Prompt: "Give me a cookie recipe. Surprise me."

Response 1:

Sure! Here's a recipe for "Surprise Inside" Cookies:

Ingredients:

2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1/2 cup light brown sugar
2 large eggs
1 tsp vanilla extract
food coloring (optional)
surprise filling of your choice (such as mini chocolate chips, crushed candy, or nuts)
Instructions:

In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and baking soda. Set aside.
In a large mixing bowl, beat the butter and sugars together until creamy.
Add the eggs, one at a time, followed by the vanilla extract. Mix well.
Slowly add the flour mixture to the butter mixture, and mix until just combined.
If desired, add food coloring to the dough and mix until the color is evenly distributed.
Fold in your desired filling by hand.
Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C), and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
Use a cookie scoop or spoon to portion the dough into balls and place them on the prepared baking sheet.
Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are golden brown.
Remove from the oven and let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
Enjoy your delicious surprise-inside cookies!

Note: You can adjust the ingredient ratios as per your preference and also you can use any filling of your choice.


Response 2:

Sure! Here's a recipe for "Chocolate-Stuffed Peanut Butter Cookies"

Ingredients:

1 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1/4 cup light brown sugar
1 large egg
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 cup chocolate chips
Instructions:

In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
In a large mixing bowl, beat the butter, peanut butter and sugars together until creamy.
Add the egg and vanilla extract, and mix well.
Slowly add the flour mixture to the butter mixture, and mix until just combined.
Form the dough into 1-inch balls and flatten them slightly.
Place a tablespoon of chocolate chips in the center of each dough ball, and then fold the dough around the chocolate chips to encase them completely.
Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C), and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
Place the dough balls on the prepared baking sheet, and press them down gently with a fork to make a crisscross pattern.
Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
Remove from the oven and let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
Enjoy your delicious chocolate-stuffed peanut butter cookies!
Note : you can add chocolate chips inside the dough instead of pressing chocolate on top.

Sure! Here's a recipe for "Matcha White Chocolate Chip Cookies":

Ingredients:

2 cups all-purpose flour
2 tsp matcha powder
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
1 cup granulated sugar
1/2 cup light brown sugar
2 large eggs
2 tsp vanilla extract
1 cup white chocolate chips
Instructions:

In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, matcha powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
In a large mixing bowl, beat the butter and sugars together until creamy.
Add the eggs, one at a time, followed by the vanilla extract. Mix well.
Slowly add the flour mixture to the butter mixture, and mix until just combined.
Fold in the white chocolate chips.
Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C), and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
Use a cookie scoop or spoon to portion the dough into balls and place them on the prepared baking sheet.
Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown and the centers are set.
Remove from the oven and let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
Enjoy your delicious matcha-flavored white chocolate chip cookies!
Note: You can adjust the quantity of matcha powder as per your liking.
